EMILY DICKINSON

THE SOUL SELECTS HER OWN SOCEITY






























THE SOUL SELECTS HER OWN
SOCIETY, THEN SHUTS THE
DOOR; ON HER DIVINE
MAJORITY OBTRUDE NO MORE.
UNMOVED, SHE NOTES THE
CHARIOT'S PAUSING AT HER
LOW GATE; UNMOVED, AN
EMPEROR IS KNEELING UPON
HER MAT.I'VE KNOWN HER FROM
AN AMPLE NATION CHOOSE ONE;
THEN CLOSE THE VALVES OF HER
ATTENTION LIKE STONE.
